# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want MiniVue to be a friendly place to learn how a framework works. We
pledge to make participation a harassment-free experience for everyone,
regardless of experience level, background, or identity.

## Our standards

Examples of behavior that helps:

- Being patient with beginners — this project exists for people learning.
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback.
- Focusing on what is best for readers and learners.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, insults, or derogatory comments.
- Publishing others' private information without permission.
- Dismissiveness toward newcomers' questions.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive behavior may be reported to the maintainer at
**sirserik@gmail.com**. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated and
will result in a response appropriate to the circumstances.

This Code of Conduct is adapted from the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org),
version 2.1.
